I am currently a postdoc at COSIC, KU Leuven. My research interests are efficient algorithms for mathematical problems. Currently, I study the cryptanalysis of post-quantum cryptographic algorithms, in particular multivariate signature schemes, using computer algebra tools.

I have shown the insecurity of some NIST candidates by demonstrating practical attacks that run in seconds on a laptop, and more theoretical results that improve attacks by exponential factors. All the algorithms I design are implemented and tested, with practical and reproducible results.
